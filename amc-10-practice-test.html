AMC 10Competition directions

Students will have 75 minutes to complete the competition at the time designated by their Competition Manager.

We recommend taking the competition on an updated version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge on a tablet, laptop, or desktop device—not a mobile phone.

Do not start or open the competition in more than one window, as your answers may be overwritten.

Double-check that your computer is plugged in and set to stay awake for the duration of the competition. Good luck!

Competition Format and Allowed Aids

The AMC 10 is a 25-question, 75-minute competition. For each question, only one answer choice is correct.

Scoring: You will receive 6 points for each correct answer, 1.5 points for each problem left unanswered, and 0 points for each incorrect answer.

During the competition, students are only allowed to use:

  • Writing utensils
  • Blank scratch paper
  • Rulers
  • Compasses
  • Erasers
  • One computer with one browser tab open, displaying the loaded AMC

Prohibited materials include additional electronic devices, calculators, protractors, and graph paper. No problem requires a calculator.

Important: Do not open the competition in more than one window. Refreshing may reset unsaved submissions. If your timer freezes, write your answers on scratch paper before refreshing.

Figures are not necessarily drawn to scale. Once you submit or time runs out, your entered answers will be scored and you will proceed to the post-competition review.

If there is an urgent need to use the bathroom, all materials should remain at your working space in view of the proctor. No other breaks are permitted.

Entering Your Answers

All answers are multiple-choice selections A, B, C, D, or E. You may change your selected answer until final submission.

Reporting Competition Issues

If you run into an issue with the original competition platform, email amctech@maa.org. For this Saintly practice environment, return to the year selector and restart the practice.

Starting the Competition

When you’re ready, click Start competition. The 75-minute clock begins immediately.

Congratulations on taking the AMC 10! Good luck!

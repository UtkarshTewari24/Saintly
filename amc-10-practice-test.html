AMC 10Competition directions

You will have 75 minutes to complete the competition once you press start.

We recommend a tablet, laptop, or desktop — not a phone. Do not open the competition in more than one window, and keep your device awake for the full duration.

Format and Scoring

The AMC 10 is a 25-question, 75-minute multiple-choice competition.

Scoring: 6 points per correct answer, 1.5 points per question left blank, 0 points per wrong answer.

Allowed: writing utensils, blank scratch paper, rulers, compasses, erasers. No calculators — no problem requires one.

Heads up: refreshing may reset your answers. If the timer freezes, jot your answers on scratch paper before refreshing. The AI coach is locked during timed competitions — it comes back for the review.

Figures are not necessarily drawn to scale. When you submit (or time expires), you'll be scored and any misses go straight to your mistake log.

Starting the Competition

When you're ready, click Start competition. The 75-minute clock begins immediately. Good luck on the AMC 10!
